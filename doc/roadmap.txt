Movie-server Roadmap
====================

Movie-server is currently at version 0.5. The following represents
plans for movie-server in coming versions.

Movie-server 0.6
----------------

* Eliminate current modularity
  * Modularity will be added back, using a better HTML template
    library, when the switch to Flask occurs.
* Richer MPD control --- allow changing songs, showing song durations,
  and better guessing of current playlist.
* Hegemony support --- synchronized streaming to multiple servers
* Command-line interface
